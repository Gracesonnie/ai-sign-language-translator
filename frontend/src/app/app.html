How It Works

Three simple steps to real-time translation

No downloads, no interpreters. Just you, your camera, and instant understanding.

01
🎥

Open your camera

Click "Launch Translation Hub" and grant camera access. Your browser does the rest — no plugins or downloads required.

02
🤟

Show your sign

Make a sign language gesture in front of your camera. Our AI instantly recognizes your hand shape and movement.

03
💬

See it translated

The sign appears as text in the conversation. The support agent sees your message and can respond verbally or in text.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is SignBridge Pro free to use?
Yes! Our basic translation service is completely free for personal and small business use.
❓ What languages/signs are supported?
Currently we support ASL (American Sign Language) with plans to add more sign languages soon.
❓ Do I need special hardware?
No — just a standard webcam and a modern browser. Works on any device with a camera.
