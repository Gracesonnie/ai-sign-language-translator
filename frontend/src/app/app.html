How It Works

Three simple steps to real-time translation

No downloads, no interpreters. Just you, your camera, and instant understanding.

Step 1

Open your camera

Click "Launch Translation Hub" and grant camera access. Your browser does the rest.

Step 2

Show your sign

Make a sign language gesture in front of your camera. Our AI instantly recognizes your hand.

Step 3

See it translated

The sign appears as text in the conversation. The support agent sees your message instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SignBridge Pro free to use?
Yes! Our basic translation service is completely free for personal and small business use.
What languages/signs are supported?
Currently we support KSL (Kenyan Sign Language) with plans to add more sign languages soon.
Do I need special hardware?
No — just a standard webcam and a modern browser. Works on any device with a camera.
